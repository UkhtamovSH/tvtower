About Us

The Koinot restaurant offers its visitors fresh dishes of international and Uzbek cuisine. Getting into this restaurant, you practically make a sightseeing tour of Tashkent, as the restaurant is located at an altitude of 105 meters. You can enjoy breakfast while looking out over the city in the morning or dine at sunset with a special charm. The restaurant has piano music and an atmosphere of elegance.
