About Us

The Koinot restaurant offers its visitors fresh dishes of international and Uzbek cuisine. Getting into this restaurant, you practically make a sightseeing tour of Tashkent, as the restaurant is located at an altitude of 105 meters. You can enjoy breakfast while looking out over the city in the morning or dine at sunset with a special charm. The restaurant has piano music and an atmosphere of elegance.

Our Pride

The Highest

The Tashkent TV Tower is the tallest structure with an open observation deck in Central Asia. Our TV tower is the 13th tallest TV tower in the world with a height of 375 meters (at the time of commissioning - 9th in the world)

Wall of History

A huge panel with an area of ​​more than 255 m2. Its scale is impressive and corresponds to the grandiose idea of ​​A. BUKHARBAEV, a member of the Union of Artists. This work of monumental art is composed of compositions. Homeland, space, communications, radio television Bayram
